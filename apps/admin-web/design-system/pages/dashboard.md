## Goal

Give operators a fast read on what needs attention now.

## Layout Overrides

- Lead with one operational hero, not a marketing banner.
- Keep KPI cards compact and comparable.
- Split the lower area into:
  - upcoming bookings
  - agent traffic
  - quick actions / package pulse
- Keep navigation and signed-in context visible on desktop.

## Tone

- Operational
- Calm but alert
- Clear state emphasis over decoration
